Voom transcribes your recordings entirely on-device using WhisperKit, an Apple Silicon-optimized implementation of OpenAI's Whisper model.

## How Transcription Works

Transcription runs locally on your Mac:

- **Model:** WhisperKit distil-large-v3 (distilled version of Whisper Large v3)
- **Speed:** Approximately 1-3x realtime on Apple Silicon
- **Privacy:** Audio never leaves your machine—fully offline
- **Languages:** Supports 99+ languages (auto-detected)

<Note>
  The distil-large-v3 model is downloaded automatically on first use. Download size is approximately 1.5 GB.
</Note>

## Automatic Transcription

By default, Voom transcribes recordings with audio immediately after capture:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Recording completes">
    When you stop recording, Voom saves the video file
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="WhisperKit loads">
    The transcription model loads (takes ~5-10 seconds on first run)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Audio extraction">
    Voom extracts audio from the video and converts it to 16kHz mono WAV (WhisperKit's required format)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Transcription runs">
    WhisperKit processes the audio and returns timestamped segments
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="AI title & summary (optional)">
    If Apple Foundation Models are available (macOS 26.0+), Voom generates a descriptive title and summary from the transcript
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Auto-transcription runs in the background. You can continue using Voom while transcription is in progress.
</Tip>

### Disabling Auto-Transcription

To disable automatic transcription:

1. Open **Settings** from the menu bar
2. Go to **General** tab
3. Toggle off **"Automatically transcribe recordings"**

You can still manually transcribe recordings later from the library.

## Manual Transcription

To transcribe a recording manually:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recording">
    Select the recording in your library to open the player view
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Transcribe">
    Click the **Transcribe** button (text bubble icon) in the action bar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for completion">
    A progress indicator shows transcription status. This takes 1-5 minutes for typical recordings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Transcript Features

### Timestamped Segments

Each transcript segment includes:

- **Start time** — When the speaker begins talking (in seconds)
- **End time** — When the segment ends
- **Text** — The transcribed speech

Segments are displayed in a synchronized list alongside the video player.

### Synced Playback

The transcript automatically highlights the current segment during playback:

- **Active highlighting** — Current segment highlights as the video plays
- **Click to seek** — Click any segment to jump to that timestamp
- **Live captions** — Toggle captions to see text overlaid on the video

<Tip>
  Use captions when sharing your screen to make recordings more accessible.
</Tip>

### Editing Transcripts

You can correct transcription errors:

1. **Double-click** any segment to edit its text
2. Type your correction
3. Press **Enter** to save

Edits are saved immediately and sync with the shared link (if you've already uploaded).

### Searching Transcripts

Use the search bar above the transcript to find specific words or phrases:

- Type in the search field to filter segments
- Matching segments are highlighted
- Click a result to jump to that moment in the video

<Note>
  Search also works in the library view—you can search across all your recordings' transcripts at once.
</Note>

## AI-Generated Metadata

### Title Generation

If Apple Foundation Models are available on your Mac (macOS 26.0+), Voom automatically generates a descriptive title from the transcript:

- Replaces the default filename-based title
- Summarizes the recording's content in 3-8 words
- Can be manually edited by clicking the title field

### Summary Generation

Voom also generates a brief summary:

- 1-3 sentence overview of the recording
- Appears in the player view below the video
- Editable via the summary text area

<Warning>
  AI title and summary generation requires Apple Silicon (M1 or newer) with macOS 26.0+ and Apple Foundation Models available. If unavailable, recordings keep their filename-based title.
</Warning>

### Regenerating Metadata

To regenerate the title and summary:

1. Right-click the recording in the library
2. Select **"Regenerate Title & Summary"**
3. Voom re-analyzes the transcript and updates both fields

## Audio Requirements

### Supported Sources

Transcription works with:

- Recordings with **microphone audio** only
- Recordings with **system audio** only
- Recordings with **both sources** (mixed into one track)

### Unsupported Cases

<Warning>
  Transcription requires an audio track. It will not run on:
</Warning>

- Screen-only recordings (no mic, no system audio)
- Recordings where the audio track is missing or corrupted

## Performance

### Transcription Speed

On Apple Silicon Macs:

- **M1/M2:** ~1-2x realtime (5-minute recording = 2-5 minutes to transcribe)
- **M3/M4:** ~2-3x realtime (5-minute recording = 1.5-2.5 minutes)

<Tip>
  Transcription is CPU/GPU intensive. Close other apps if transcription is slow or your Mac is thermal throttling.
</Tip>

### First-Run Setup

The first time you transcribe:

1. WhisperKit downloads the distil-large-v3 model (~1.5 GB)
2. Model is cached in `~/Library/Caches/huggingface/` for future use
3. Subsequent transcriptions skip the download and start immediately

## Accuracy Tips

To improve transcription quality:

- **Use a good microphone** — Built-in Mac mics work, but external USB/XLR mics are clearer
- **Reduce background noise** — Close windows, mute notifications, avoid noisy environments
- **Speak clearly** — Pause between thoughts to create natural segment breaks
- **Avoid music/overlapping voices** — Whisper struggles with multiple simultaneous speakers

## Transcript Export

Transcripts are included when you:

- **Share via link** — Uploaded to Cloudflare R2 as JSON, displayed on the share page with synced highlighting
- **Export the recording** — Stored in the `.mov` file's metadata (accessible via Voom)

<Note>
  There's no dedicated "export transcript" feature yet. Copy/paste from the transcript view if you need plain text.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Transcription fails with 'No audio track found'">
    This recording has no audio. Enable **System Audio** or **Microphone** when recording to get transcripts.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Transcription is very slow">
    Check Activity Monitor for CPU/GPU usage. Close other intensive apps. On Intel Macs, WhisperKit may not work at all (requires Apple Silicon).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Transcript is gibberish or incorrect language">
    WhisperKit auto-detects language. If it guesses wrong, the transcript may be nonsense. Currently no manual language override—this is a known limitation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Model download fails">
    Check your internet connection. WhisperKit downloads from Hugging Face. If blocked (corporate firewall), transcription won't work. Manual model download is not yet supported.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No AI title/summary generated">
    AI title and summary generation requires macOS 26.0+ with Apple Foundation Models available. This feature is only available on newer macOS versions with Apple Silicon.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
