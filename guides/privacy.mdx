## Local-First by Design

Voom is built with privacy as the default. **All recordings stay on your Mac** unless you explicitly choose to share them.

<Card title="Key Privacy Principle" icon="shield-check">
  Your recordings, transcripts, and metadata never leave your device unless you opt-in to cloud sharing.
</Card>

## What Stays Local

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Screen Recordings" icon="video">
    All video files are stored locally on your Mac in HEVC format using hardware-accelerated encoding. Recordings are saved to your local file system and are never uploaded automatically.
    
    **Location**: Managed by the app's local storage service (`Services/Storage/`)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Transcriptions" icon="closed-captioning">
    Transcription happens entirely on-device using **WhisperKit** (distil-large-v3 model). The audio never leaves your machine.
    
    - Runs on Apple Silicon neural engine
    - Fully offline — no internet required
    - Transcript data stored locally alongside recordings
    
    **Technology**: `Services/Transcription/` using WhisperKit framework
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Metadata" icon="database">
    Recording metadata (title, date, duration, transcript) is stored locally on your Mac.
    
    - No analytics sent to external servers
    - No tracking of recording habits
    - No telemetry or crash reporting
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Cloud Sharing (Opt-In)

Cloud sharing is **completely optional** and requires manual setup. When you choose to share a recording:

<Steps>
  <Step title="You Control the Infrastructure">
    Shared videos are uploaded to **your own Cloudflare account**, not Voom's servers. You own the infrastructure:
    
    - Cloudflare R2 for video storage
    - Cloudflare D1 for metadata
    - Cloudflare Workers for the share page API
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explicit Per-Recording Consent">
    Sharing is per-recording, not automatic. You choose which videos to upload.
  </Step>

  <Step title="30-Day Auto-Expiry">
    Share links automatically expire after 30 days (configurable). A daily cron job cleans up expired videos and metadata.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Gets Uploaded

When you share a recording, the following is uploaded to **your Cloudflare account**:

- Video file (HEVC encoded)
- Transcript data (for synced captions)
- Basic metadata (title, duration, upload timestamp)

### What Doesn't Get Uploaded

- Your personal information
- Recording history or library contents
- Analytics or viewing data
- Any recordings you don't explicitly share

<Note>
  The Cloudflare Worker code is open source. You can audit exactly what data is stored and how it's handled in the `voom-share/` directory.
</Note>

## Share Page Privacy

The share page is designed with viewer privacy in mind:

| Feature | Privacy Benefit |
|---------|----------------|
| View-only | No download button — reduces unauthorized redistribution |
| No tracking | No analytics or viewer tracking |
| No login required | Viewers don't need accounts |
| Expiring links | 30-day auto-deletion limits exposure window |

<Warning>
  While the share page is view-only, determined viewers could still capture video content using screen recording tools. Don't share sensitive content you wouldn't want potentially copied.
</Warning>

## Credentials & Secrets

Voom follows security best practices for handling credentials:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="App Storage" icon="lock">
    Cloud sharing credentials (Worker URL and API secret) are stored in macOS UserDefaults. They never leave your device.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Worker Deployment" icon="cloud">
    The API secret is deployed using `npx wrangler secret put API_SECRET` and stored securely in Cloudflare's environment.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Setting Up Cloud Sharing

Credentials are configured locally:

1. Right-click the Voom menu bar icon
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Enter your Worker URL and API secret
4. These are stored locally and used only when you choose to share

## Cost of Cloud Sharing

Because you use your own Cloudflare infrastructure, costs are transparent:

- **~$0.35/month** for 2-3 videos per day
- R2 storage pricing: $0.015/GB stored per month
- Workers requests: 100,000 free requests/day
- D1 database: 5GB storage included free

<Tip>
  You can monitor exact costs in your Cloudflare dashboard. No surprise bills or hidden fees.
</Tip>

## GDPR & Compliance

Since recordings stay local by default:

- No data processing agreements needed for local use
- You control data residency for shared videos (choose Cloudflare region)
- No data shared with third parties
- Easy data deletion (delete local recordings or purge R2 bucket)

<Info>
  If you use Voom in an enterprise context with cloud sharing, consult your legal team about data handling policies for your Cloudflare deployment.
</Info>

## Summary

Voom's privacy model is simple:

1. **Everything local by default** — recordings, transcripts, metadata
2. **On-device transcription** — WhisperKit runs entirely on your Mac
3. **Opt-in cloud sharing** — you choose what to upload, when, and where
4. **You own the infrastructure** — your Cloudflare account, your control
5. **Open source** — audit the code yourself

No tracking. No telemetry. No third-party servers. Just your recordings, on your machine.
