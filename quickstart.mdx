Get up and running with Voom in minutes. This guide walks you through installing the app, granting permissions, and creating your first screen recording with transcription.

## Install Voom

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and install">
    Download the latest release from [GitHub](https://github.com/aritropaul/voom/releases) and drag Voom.app to your Applications folder.
    
    ```bash
    open -a Voom
    ```
    
    Voom runs as a menu bar app — look for the icon in your top menu bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Grant permissions">
    On first launch, macOS requests permissions:
    
    - **Screen Recording** — Required to capture your screen
    - **Microphone** — Optional, for voice recording
    - **Camera** — Optional, for webcam overlay
    
    Click **Allow** for each prompt. You may need to restart Voom after granting permissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Voom requires macOS 15.0+ and Apple Silicon for on-device transcription.
</Note>

## Record your first video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start recording">
    Press **⌘⇧R** (Command+Shift+R) or click the Voom menu bar icon and select **Start Recording**.
    
    <Accordion title="First-time setup">
      The first time you record, Voom displays a countdown and asks you to:
      
      - Choose which display to record
      - Enable/disable webcam overlay
      - Enable/disable system audio
      - Enable/disable microphone
      
      These settings are remembered for future recordings.
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Record your screen">
    After the 3-second countdown, Voom begins recording:
    
    - Your screen is captured at full resolution
    - The webcam appears as a draggable overlay (if enabled)
    - System audio and mic are recorded simultaneously
    
    Click **Stop** in the menu bar or press **⌘⇧R** again to finish.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View your recording">
    Press **⌘⇧L** (Command+Shift+L) to open the Library window.
    
    Your recording appears at the top with:
    - Auto-generated title (if Apple Intelligence is enabled)
    - Duration and resolution
    - Creation date
    
    Click the recording to play it back with the built-in player.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enable transcription

Voom can transcribe your recordings on-device using WhisperKit. The first transcription downloads the ML model (~1.5GB).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable auto-transcription">
    Click the Voom menu bar icon → **Settings** → **General** tab.
    
    Enable **Auto-transcribe new recordings**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Record with transcription">
    Create a new recording using **⌘⇧R**.
    
    After recording stops, Voom automatically transcribes the audio. This takes ~30 seconds per minute of recording on Apple Silicon.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View the transcript">
    Open your recording in the Library (**⌘⇧L**).
    
    The transcript appears below the video player with:
    - Timestamped segments
    - Click-to-seek (click any line to jump to that moment)
    - Full-text search
    
    <Tip>
      Edit transcript text by double-clicking any segment. Changes are saved automatically.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Share your recording (optional)

To share recordings via link, you need to set up cloud hosting on Cloudflare. This is entirely optional — recordings work locally without it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy Cloudflare Worker">
    Follow the [cloud sharing setup guide](/self-hosting/cloudflare-setup) to deploy the Worker to your Cloudflare account.
    
    Total cost: ~$0.35/month for 2-3 videos per day.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Voom">
    In Voom settings (**Settings** → **Sharing** tab):
    
    - **Worker URL**: Your Cloudflare Worker URL
    - **API Secret**: The secret you set with `wrangler secret put API_SECRET`
    
    Click **Test Connection** to verify.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Share a recording">
    In the Library, right-click any recording and select **Share**.
    
    Voom uploads the video and transcript to your Cloudflare R2 bucket and returns a shareable link:
    
    ```
    https://your-worker.workers.dev/s/abc123xyz
    ```
    
    Links expire after 30 days by default.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Keyboard shortcuts

Master these shortcuts for the fastest workflow:

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **⌘⇧R** | Start/stop recording |
| **⌘⇧L** | Open library window |
| **Space** | Play/pause video |
| **←/→** | Seek backward/forward 5 seconds |
| **⌘⌫** | Delete selected recording |

See the [full keyboard shortcuts reference](/app/keyboard-shortcuts) for more.
